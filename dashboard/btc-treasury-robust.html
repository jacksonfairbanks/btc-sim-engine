What This Means
Of 5,000 simulated 4-year paths...
  • 89.2% experience a drawdown of 50% or more at some point — this is normal BTC behavior, not a tail event.
  • 23.5% experience a drawdown of 75% or more — comparable to the 2018 and 2022 bear markets.
  • 70.9% spend at least one continuous year below their all-time high — extended drawdown durations are the norm, not the exception.
These are not hypothetical scenarios imposed on the model — they emerge naturally from resampling actual historical BTC return sequences. The block bootstrap cannot generate return patterns that never occurred. Every simulated crash is built from real crash blocks.
