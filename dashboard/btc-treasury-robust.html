Methodology

Models

ModelTypeWalk-Forward ScoreDescription
RBBNonparametric0.8109Stationary block bootstrap. Resamples actual historical BTC return sequences. Geometric block length (30d mean). No regime switching.
GARCH(1,1)Parametric0.7800Volatility clustering model. Student-t innovations (nu=3.28). Captures big-moves-follow-big-moves but generates one day at a time.

Validation

9 scoring metrics (path dynamics 35%, tail risk 25%, distributional 15%, vol clustering 10%, forecast 15%). Walk-forward validated across 126 out-of-sample windows with exponential recency weighting (2-year half-life). RBB outperforms GARCH by +0.031 and GBM by +0.048.

BCR Solvency Logic

BCR = BTC reserves ($) / annual dividend. Monthly: sell BTC to meet dividend obligation, recompute BCR. Failure = BCR < 1 at any point. BCR is the only driver — amplification and dividend rate collapse into BCR. Dollar amounts are presentation-layer only.

Data

BTC daily OHLCV from 2013 to present (Yahoo Finance). Models trained on full history. 5,000 paths x 1,460 days (4 years) per model.
